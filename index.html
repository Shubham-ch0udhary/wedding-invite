Our Love Story

College Days

2014-2018

Shubham first met Jayshree during a debate competition audition, where he was impressed by her exceptional oratory skills. Though his only wish was to genuinely befriend her, devoid of any ulterior motives, it seemed too far fetched at the time. However, as fate would have it, they were eventually introduced through a mutual friend, and their paths crossed, leading to them becoming acquaintances during their college days.

When we weren't unemployed anymore

Over time, the bond matured, and acquaintances became close friends. The addition of new salaries to their accounts empowered these individuals to plan numerous fantastic group trips and without disrespecting the friendship vow (a vow made during their friendship stating 'if we don't get married till 30, we will have each other's back), these kids also pledged on something as lame as this vow.

Dating

Few years later, their friendship advanced into a love story penned by destiny, proving that sometimes, the best love stories are the ones written by lifelong friends who dared to venture beyond the margins of friendship and into the sweet pages of romance. Here's to the couple who turned friendship into forever – a love story as timeless as the knowledge gained in those college hallways."

Proposal

Shubham, with his great sense of all things filmy, planned a surprise proposal that shocked Jayshree. He gathered friends and family to witness the pivotal moment. With bated breath, he knelt down and asked Jayshree the most profound question yet the easiest one she had to answer, "Miss Chatterjee, Kya aap apni zindagi ke kuch pal yahi kuch 60-70 saal mere sath bitana chahengi ? Tears flowed freely, not just from Jayshree but also from Shubham, their friends, and probably anyone with a heart. It was an emotional affair, followed by a blend of singing, dancing, and an abundance of joy."
